Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How can I track my order?

After checkout you would normally receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Since this is an educational demo, tracking is not available.

What is your return policy?

In a real store we’d offer a 30-day return policy for unworn and unwashed items. For this demo, no returns are processed.

Do you ship internationally?

Shipping options would typically depend on your location. For this demo website, no shipping is available.

How do I care for StyleHub clothing?

Care instructions are usually included on the garment label. As a general rule, wash cold and air dry to extend the life of clothing.
